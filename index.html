About Me

I am a final year Computer Science student at Vellore Institute of Technology. I was an academic intern at the National University of Singapore during which I worked on an NLP project to detect misinformation. In VIT-REX Cyber Forensics lab I experimented with OSINT tools and made a model that can detect fake job postings. Now, I am working as an Integrations Intern at Darwinbox where I am making API connectors using R.

I strongly believe that Machine Learning can aid in making impactful decisions in the healthcare industry. I am working on how to detect neurodegenerative diseases earlier. I want to learn more about making interpretable models that fit the use cases while balancing their complexity and accuracy.
